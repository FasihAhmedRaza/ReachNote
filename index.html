Your Privacy is Fully Protected

Reach Note is a completely offline application. All your notes, checklists, reminders, and budgets are stored exclusively on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and we collect zero personal information — ever.

1. Overview

Reach Note ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal productivity application that helps you capture, manage, and remember important information. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

We are fully committed to protecting your privacy. In short: we collect nothing, store nothing externally, and share nothing.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Reach Note does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data sent to external servers

There are no servers. Reach Note is a fully offline application.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All notes, checklists, budgets, categories, and reminder settings you create are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage).

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any cloud service by the app itself (though your device's own backup system, such as Google Backup, may include it as part of app data).

You can permanently delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app.

4. Notifications & Reminders

Reach Note uses local notifications to deliver reminders you explicitly set. These notifications:

  • Are scheduled entirely on your device
  • Do not require an internet connection
  • Are not processed by any external server
  • Can be cancelled at any time by marking a note as done or by turning off notifications in your device settings

5. Location Feature

You may optionally type a location label on any note (e.g., "G Mart" or "Level 3 Parking"). This is plain text stored locally on your device.

When you tap a location, the app opens your device's native Maps application (Google Maps or Apple Maps). This action transfers only the text label to that external app. Reach Note does not access your GPS or device location at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

Reach Note does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The app is built on the Expo / React Native framework. Expo may collect minimal anonymous diagnostic data about development tools during development builds only. This is completely separate from user data and does not apply to production/release builds.

7. Children's Privacy

Reach Note does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

We recommend keeping your device secured and up to date. We are not responsible for data accessed by other apps or persons with physical access to your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and communicated through app updates.

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
